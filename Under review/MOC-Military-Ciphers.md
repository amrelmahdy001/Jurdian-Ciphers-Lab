## What Makes a Cipher "Military"?

Military ciphers were designed for high-stakes operational security under field conditions. They needed to be:
- **Fast** to encipher and decipher manually or mechanically
- **Secure** against an enemy with captured material and significant analytical resources
- **Robust** against transmission errors (common in wartime radio)
- **Practical** for use by trained but not necessarily expert operators
