# Historical & Unsolved Ciphers — Index

> **Definition:** This index covers famous historical cipher *instances* — specific encrypted messages or documents, as distinct from the cipher *algorithms* used to create them.
> ← Back to [[MOC-Ciphers]]

---

## Important Distinction

This section is about **specific encrypted artifacts**, not cipher algorithms:

- [[Caesar Cipher]] is a cipher *algorithm* → lives in [[MOC-Shift-Ciphers]]
- A specific Caesar-encrypted message from Julius Caesar would be a *historical cipher instance* → lives here

Most historical cipher instances use known algorithms applied to real historical messages. The puzzle is the specific content, context, and key — not the algorithm itself.

## Solved Historical Ciphers

### Medieval & Renaissance
- [[Mary Stuart Code]] — Nomenclator cipher used by Mary Queen of Scots; broken by Thomas Phelippes, 1586; led to her execution
- [[Babington Plot Cipher]] — Related to Mary Stuart; homophonic nomenclator

### 18th–19th Century
- [[Arnold Cipher]] — Numeric cipher used by Benedict Arnold and John André; American Revolutionary War, 1780
- [[Beale Ciphers]] — Three ciphertexts; one solved (book cipher using Declaration of Independence); two remain unsolved
- [[Gold Bug Cipher]] — Fictional symbolic cipher from Edgar Allan Poe's 1843 story; solvable within the story

### 20th Century
- [[Kryptos]] — Sculpture by Jim Sanborn at CIA headquarters; four sections; three solved, one (K4) remains unsolved

## Unsolved Ciphers

> [!warning] Genuinely Unsolved
> The following ciphers have resisted expert cryptanalysis for decades or centuries. They may contain an encrypted message, an undiscovered algorithm, or may be hoaxes.

- [[Voynich Manuscript]] — 15th-century illustrated manuscript; unknown language or script; unknown author; no consensus on whether it encodes a real language
- [[Dorabella Cipher]] — 87-character symbol cipher sent by Edward Elgar to Dora Penny, 1897; never solved
- [[Zodiac Killer Cipher]] — Four cryptograms sent by the Zodiac Killer; Z408 solved; Z340 solved 2020; Z13 and Z32 remain unsolved
- [[Beale Ciphers]] (B1 and B3) — Two of three Beale papers remain unsolved
- [[McCormick Cipher]] — Found with the body of Ricky McCormick, 1999; two encrypted notes; FBI unable to solve
- [[Tamam Shud Case]] — Mysterious death in Australia, 1948; encrypted note in clothing; unsolved
